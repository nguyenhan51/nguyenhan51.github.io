Digital Image Composition

Photoshop & Illustrator, Feb 11, 2020

Night Ocean

I made this composition an artwork where the girl is drawed in flat color using pen tool and gradient in Illustrator. Then I used Photoshop to add elements and background using blend mode and mask. I also adjust the hue, saturation and lighting,... to make the whole piece has the dark blue color. I learned more ways to blend images together and apply to blend the ocean foam with the starry night sky to the background.



Glitch Art

Audacity & txt, Feb 18, 2020

I used my own composition image and used 3 different effects: Echo, Distortion and Invert in Audacity to create the glitch image. As for the final result, I used the online "Image Glitch Tool" to add the extreme glitch effect for the image. The colors in this work are all distorted and the picture becomes very pixelated but some of same elements from the original image are still recognizable, which I think creates mysterious and interesting theme to the image.
