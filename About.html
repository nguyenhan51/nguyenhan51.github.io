Artist Statement



I have been always interested in art when I was small. Being to express myself freely without using words through different types and forms of artworks feel very special and extrodinary for me. At first, I did art just as an hobby because I like drawing and painting, but as I continue to make art, I realize that I'm developing a bigger passion for art than just a hobby.


I started exploring and creating traditional illustrations with different mediums like: watercolor, gouche, oil, mixed media,... when I was in highschool. I didn't focus much on digital art until recently as I'm still learning and improving. In Art 74 course, I'm able to exposed to new media art and get to learn how to make my own works using programs and techniques like: Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator, Audacity, Meshmixer,...
